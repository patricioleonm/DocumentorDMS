Manage Subscriptions

This page displays the list of folders and documents for which you are a subscriber. Subscriptions may be added under Browse Collections->Add Folder Subscription. Subscriptions may also be added on a per-document basis by browsing to the document and clicking the subscribe button.

To cancel a subscription:

  1. Check the folder(s)/document(s) you wish to unsubscribe from
  2. Click the Unsubscribe button.
  3. You will be prompted to confirm your selection.
