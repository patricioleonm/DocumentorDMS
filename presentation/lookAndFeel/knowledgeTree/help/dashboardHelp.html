Dashboard

The Dashboard is divided into five sections:

  1. Dashboard News. KnowledgeTree news items containing relevant information for the user community.
  2. Pending documents, or documents that have yet to be approved.
  3. Checked-out documents, or documents that have been checked-out by the user.
  4. Subscriptions Alerts. The user may subscribe to alerts on folders or on documents. Details of any changes to these folders/documents, such as additions, deletions or modifications, will be displayed in the Subscriptions Alerts section.
  5. Quick links to useful web pages.
